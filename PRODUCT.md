# Product

## Register

product

## Users

HSK 1–4 learners globally, beginner to intermediate. They open Moink in short daily sessions: commuting, before bed, a spare 10 minutes between tasks. They want real progress on vocabulary and cultural fluency, not badge collection. Many juggle work or school alongside study. Some are heritage learners reconnecting with roots; most are enthusiastic newcomers discovering the language for the first time.

## Product Purpose

Moink is a Chinese learning platform that combines structured vocabulary study with cultural exploration. It groups vocabulary by shared radicals, animates stroke order, offers quizzes and flashcards, and connects words to real cultural contexts: destinations, food, festivals. A learner finishing a session should feel sharper and more culturally alive, not just less guilty about skipping practice.

There is also a brand landing surface (marketing, onboarding). PRODUCT.md registers `product` as the primary default; use `brand` register override when working on the landing page.

## Brand Personality

Playful, focused, rooted. The mascot (Moink, a red panda) carries the playfulness. The vocabulary and culture content grounds it in something real. The tool interface respects the user's intelligence. Fun enough to return to daily; precise enough to actually teach.

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS**: teal-white-rounded, hero metrics, identical card grids, dashboard clichés. Moink is a tool, not a B2B product.
- **Academic/sterile**: textbook grey, dense unlabeled tables, no personality. Learning Chinese is not like reading a grammar manual.
- **Duolingo-style gamification overload**: streaks exist; they do not dominate. The owl does not guilt-trip. Achievements are earned quietly.
- **Tourist-trap aesthetics**: red lanterns, dragon motifs, "orient" clichés. Cultural respect, not cultural costume.

## Design Principles

1. **Distraction-free depth**: every screen has one primary job. Vocabulary cards do not compete with streak alerts.
2. **Culture is content, not decoration**: ink textures and cultural references appear because they teach something, not as background wallpaper.
3. **Tool confidence**: the product trusts users are capable learners. No hand-holding microcopy, no excessive positive reinforcement after every card flip.
4. **Earned delight**: Moink the mascot appears with intention, as a reward or a moment, not a constant presence.
5. **Sessions end, learning continues**: the design acknowledges real-world interrupted study. Progress is persistent and visible without being anxiety-inducing.

## References

- **Anki**: focused, distraction-free study loops. No noise between you and the flashcard.
- **Linear**: sharp, fast, premium product feel. Dense information done with craft. Respects the user's intelligence.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Keyboard navigation throughout all learning flows. `prefers-reduced-motion` respected for Framer Motion animations; Hanzi stroke animations pause or use reduced paths. Color is never the sole information carrier (critical for character practice where visual distinction matters). Mobile-first: learners study on phones in varied lighting conditions.
